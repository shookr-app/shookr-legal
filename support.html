Common Questions

Where is my data stored?
All your journal entries are stored locally on your device. We don't have access to your data — it never leaves your phone.

How do I cancel my subscription?
Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name at the top, then tap Subscriptions. From there you can manage or cancel your Shookr subscription.

I lost my entries after reinstalling the app.
Because all data is stored on your device, uninstalling the app will remove your entries. We're sorry, but there is no way to recover them once the app has been removed.

How do I change my reminder time?
Go to the Profile tab in the app and tap on Reminder Time to set a new time.
